TimeSaved Privacy Policy

 

Effective January 29, 2016

 

At TimeSaved, our goal of connecting individuals (“TimeSeekers”) to to highly-skilled service providers (“TimeSavers”) requires us to collect and retain information. We take privacy seriously and will take reasonable steps to protect your personal information. This policy sets out how TimeSaved (“we” or “us”) uses, protects, and preserves information submitted by TimeSeekers and TimeSavers. By using TimeSaved and by submitting information through our website, you are agreeing to the terms of this policy (the “Policy”). The Policy should be read in conjunction with the Terms of Use available on our website, along with any other TimeSaved policies and procedure, and you should not use the website unless you fully understand and agree to the Terms of Use and the Policy. Any capitalized terms not defined herein have the same meaning as defined in the Terms of Use.

 

If you have any questions about the Policy, please contact TimeSaved by sending an email with your questions to privacy@TimeSaved.ca before entering any information on the website.

 

  1. Information We Collect and Store

 

When you choose to create an account with TimeSaved and use our website, we will collect and retain all personal information submitted to us. Personal information includes but is not necessarily limited to:

 

  • Your name;

  • Your email address;

  • Your telephone number;

  • Your mailing address;

  • Your business address;

  • Your industry and the nature of your business; and

  • Information related to requests for services, and/or your offers to provide services, as applicable.

 

We will not use this personal information to contact you for any reason unrelated to your use of TimeSaved.  Except with your consent or where required by law, we will not provide your personal information to any third-party. TimeSeekers must consent to share necessary personal information in order to facilitate that connection with TimeSavers in the manner described in our Terms of Use.

 

We will not use your personal information or any other identifying information for commercial reasons unrelated to TimeSaved.

 

We collect and retain all information you enter through our website, including the information related to specific requests for services. Information submitted to TimeSaved by TimeSavers is also collected and retained by TimeSaved.

 

TimeSavers are required to conduct themselves in compliance with applicable privacy laws; however, TimeSaved cannot directly control or be liable in any way for how TimeSavers will treat the personal information provided to them.

 

Similarly, we cannot control or dictate how any third-party application you use in conjunction with TimeSaved, including LinkedIn, Twitter, Twillio or Google, will treat your personal information. We advise you to contact those parties directly to review their privacy policies.

 

To learn about your user experience and to make periodic improvements to TimeSaved, we collect and retain information routinely collected by web browsers. We will automatically record information about your activity on the website. This may include your Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, browser type, the web pages visited before and after you came to the website, information you access and search for on the website, locale preferences, identification numbers associated with your computer or mobile devices, your mobile carrier, date and time stamps associated with transactions, system configuration information, metadata, and other interactions with TimeSaved, our website and services.

 

We use “cookies” to collect information and improve TimeSaved. A cookie is a small data file that we transfer to your computer or mobile device. We may use “persistent cookies” to save your user ID and login password for future logins to the website. We may use “session ID cookies” to enable certain features of the the website, to better understand how you interact with the website and to monitor aggregate usage and web traffic routing on the website. You can instruct your browser, by changing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all aspects of our website.

 

TimeSaved may use the data collected from “cookies” and web browsers for its own purposes, both related to and unrelated to improving our website and services. In the event we disclose this data to any third-party for any purposes, it will be used anonymously and stripped of any and all personal or identifying information.


 

  1. How We Use Your Personal Information

 

We use your personal information only for purposes of facilitating your use of our website. We do not use your personal information for any additional or unrelated commercial purposes.


 

  1. Retaining Your Information

 

If you want us to permanently delete your personal information from our servers at any time, please send an email to privacy@TimeSaved.ca. We will ensure your information is deleted in a reasonable time after receiving that request. However, deleting such information may require us to terminate your account in its entirety.  

 

So long as your account remains active, we will preserve your personal information. If you have not accessed your account for a total of seven years, we will delete your account, without notice, along with any personal information retained by us.

 

When deleting your personal information, whether by request or due to inactivity, we will use standard electronic means to remove your personal information from our files, including known backups containing personal information.

 

We will also retain any and all personal information that we are required to retain under any applicable laws for the full duration of time required under those laws.

 

 

  1. Keeping Your Information Secure

 

We will store your information only in electronic format on industry standard servers that we have selected. Our servers may be located inside or outside of Canada, and thus may be subject to the laws of other countries.  Except where required to do so by law, we will not store your personal information in any physical or paper-based format.

 

The servers we select to store information engage security software and generally accepted standards to protect personal information. The software used by these servers is intended to prevent unauthorized access or improper use.

 

However, no security system is perfect and there is always a risk of unauthorized parties accessing your personal information. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the complete protection and security of your personal information.


 

  1. Disclosure of User Information to TimeSavers

 

The personal information of TimeSeekers will be provided to TimeSavers to facilitate communication between the parties and to enable the TimeSavers to perform the requested services.

  1. TimeSaved is not Intended for Children

 

The services offered on our website by TimeSaved and TimeSavers are not intended to be accessed by children. TimeSaved does not engage in any marketing efforts aimed at children.  We therefore do not take any special precautions to protect the information of persons under the age of 18. In the event any information is submitted by a person under the age of 18, we will delete that information immediately upon being advised of that person’s true age.
