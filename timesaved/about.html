About TimeSaved

TimeSaved connects you with people nearby who can help you with your errands.

  • What

    Often in our busy lives, the things that are most important to us don’t get the attention they deserve and we just don’t know how to get everything done. TimeSaved connects those who have skills and time to share with those who need some time back for themselves.

  • Why

    We want to build a more connected Canada. We believe that technology has the power to bring people together, build stronger communities and encourage the inclusiveness we so proudly stand for. We know that entrepreneurs don't always have the resources to properly explore their passions and share their skills for the benefit of others. So we are creating a place where people can come together to get things done.

  • Who

    We provide a platform that encourages anyone with time and skills to help those around them. With the necessary tools, resources and support provided, entrepreneurs can decide when and where they want to work, how much their time is worth and what talents they're able to offer. We help build the customer/TimeSaver connections, use reviews to maintain a standard of care and process payments with an easy-to-use, secure system.

What value are we bringing?

By minimizing (and in some cases eliminating) the logistical costs of brick and mortar service providers, like storefront rental and travel time, we’re able to offer services to clients at a fraction of the usual cost. Plus, every job is reviewed by clients to assure future clients of the value of service and to help TimeSavers build their own brand. Positive reviews can help TimeSavers reach a higher earning potential while ensuring better communication, creativity and customer care.
